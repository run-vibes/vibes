### Logo

The primary logo is from **Direction B** (`12-spoke-direction-b-luxurious.html`):

**Key elements:**
- **Outer ring** â€” Gold gradient stroke
- **Inner dashed ring** â€” Subtle decorative element at radius 20
- **Hub ring** â€” Stroke ring around the core
- **Hub core** â€” Solid gold center dot
- **6 spokes** â€” Arranged at 60Â° intervals (like a clock), gradient strokes
- **Spokes end at hub ring** â€” They don't extend to the center

**Why this works:**
- 6 spokes feel more complete than 4 (compass/clock resonance)
- Dashed inner ring adds Art Deco elegance without clutter
- Hub ring + core creates depth (not just a dot)
- Gradient strokes add warmth
- The proportions are refined â€” nothing touches that shouldn't
